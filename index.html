Bio

Jessica Baldanza is an archivist and writer currently based in Toronto, Canada. She holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Ontario College of Art and Design University (2015), and a Master of Information from University of Toronto (2019) where she studied archival practice and STS (Science & Technology Studies). Jessica’s archival work centres on the management of visual media records, archives development, and the implementation and study of information and communication technologies. Her writing practice is concerned with technology-mediated intimacy.
